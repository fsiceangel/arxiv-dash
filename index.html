A Comparative Study of “Focus Contraction” in Mathematics 数学领域“聚焦收缩”的对比研究

Does mathematics show the same narrowing of collective focus that Hao, Xu, Li & Evans (Nature, 2026) documented across the natural sciences in the age of AI? Using the monthly submission record of 32 arXiv mathematics subfields (2017–2026), we test the structural analog of their thesis. We find a real concentration of mathematical activity toward computational, data-rich subfields — but formal tests place its timing in the pandemic era (2019–2021), not the LLM era: the trend plateaued before ChatGPT and shows no significant post-2022 acceleration. Mathematics, so far, is a contrast to the natural-science pattern rather than a confirmation of it.

在人工智能时代，Hao、Xu、Li 与 Evans（《自然》2026） 记录了自然科学整体研究视野的收缩。数学是否也呈现同样的趋势？我们用 2017–2026 年 32 个 arXiv 数学子领域的每月投稿数据， 检验其论点的结构性类比。我们发现数学活动确实真实地向计算型、数据密集型子领域集中——但正式统计检验表明其时间点在 疫情时期（2019–2021）而非大模型时期：该趋势在 ChatGPT 出现之前已趋于平稳，2022 年后没有显著加速。 就目前而言，数学与自然科学的模式形成对照，而非印证。

1 · The result we build on 所引用的研究结论

Hao et al. analyzed 41.3 million natural-science papers and used a language model (F1 = 0.875) to flag AI-augmented research. Their paradox: AI expands individual impact — such authors publish 3.02× more papers, earn 4.84× more citations, and lead projects 1.37 years earlier — yet AI adoption contracts collective science, shrinking the volume of topics studied by 4.63% and reducing inter-paper engagement by 22%. AI-augmented work moves toward “areas richest in data,” forming “lonely crowds” that automate established fields rather than open new ones.

该研究分析了 4130 万篇自然科学论文，用语言模型（F1 = 0.875）识别 AI 辅助研究。其悖论在于：AI 扩大了个人影响力 ——使用者发表论文数为 3.02 倍、被引为 4.84 倍、提前 1.37 年成为项目负责人——却收缩了科学整体， 使研究主题总量减少 4.63%、论文间互动减少 22%。AI 辅助研究趋向“数据最丰富的领域”，形成“孤独的人群”， 更多是自动化既有领域，而非开拓新方向。

2 · Our question & method 研究问题与方法

We cannot observe citations, authorship, or AI use from listing counts, so we test the structural signature of focus contraction: if mathematics were also narrowing, its output should concentrate across subfields over time, and growth should skew toward its most computational / data-rich areas. From the per-month counts we compute, for each year 2017–2025 (2026 partial), the distribution of submissions over the 32 subfields and four concentration measures:

  • Effective number of subfields = 2H (H = Shannon entropy, bits) — the count of equally-active subfields the field behaves like. Falling = concentrating. 有效子领域数 = 2^H（H 为香农熵）；下降表示集中。
  • Pielou evenness = H / log₂(32) — how evenly papers spread across subfields. Pielou 均匀度。
  • Top-5 share & HHI (Herfindahl index) — weight of the largest subfields. 前五份额与 HHI 集中度指数。
  • Data-cluster share — combined share of the ML-adjacent / data-rich cluster (cs.IT, math.OC, math.NA, math.ST, math.PR), the math counterpart of “areas richest in data.” 数据密集子领域（信息论、最优化、数值分析、统计、概率）的合计份额。

Description alone can’t say when the shift happened or whether it is noise, so §4 adds formal tests on the monthly series (114 points): a structural-break search that lets the data choose the break date (sup-F with block-bootstrap p-values), multinomial bootstrap confidence intervals on the annual concentration level, and a two-way fixed-effects difference-in-differences with a yearly event study for the data-rich cluster (cluster-robust SEs). 仅有描述无法说明变化何时发生、是否只是噪声，故 §4 在月度序列（114 个点）上加入正式检验： 由数据自行选择断点日期的结构性断点搜索（sup-F，块自助法 p 值）、年度集中度的多项自助置信区间， 以及针对数据密集集群的双向固定效应双重差分与逐年事件研究（按类别聚类稳健标准误）。

The signature is unmistakable though modest. The effective number of active subfields fell from 24.2 to 23.5 (≈ −3%), evenness slipped from 0.920 to 0.911, the top-5 share rose from 36.6% to 38.8%, and the data-rich cluster grew from 26.8% to 30.3% of all submissions. Growth was sharply uneven: Optimization & Control (+128%), Numerical Analysis (+97%) and Statistics Theory (+95%) — all computational — led the field, while classical/pure areas trailed (Classical Analysis & ODEs −1%, Complex Variables +26%, Operator Algebras +32%, K-Theory +30%). Whether this timing actually implicates AI is a question description can’t answer — §4 tests it formally.

信号虽温和却清晰：有效子领域数从 24.2 降至 23.5（约 −3%），均匀度由 0.920 降至 0.911，前五份额由 36.6% 升至 38.8%，数据密集集群占比由 26.8% 升至 30.3%。增长高度不均：最优化与控制（+128%）、 数值分析（+97%）、统计理论（+95%）等计算型领域领跑，而经典 / 纯数学领域明显落后 （经典分析与常微分方程 −1%、复变 +26%、算子代数 +32%、K 理论 +30%）。这一时间线是否真的指向 AI， 需要 §4 的正式检验来回答。

Interpretation / 解读. Directionally like Hao et al., at smaller magnitude: math’s collective attention has drifted toward optimization, numerics and statistics — the subfields that power, and are powered by, machine learning; the math counterpart of their “areas richest in data.” Pure analysis and algebra are not shrinking in absolute terms, but they have lost relative share. The crucial question — did this happen because of the AI era? — is answered by the tests below, and the answer is mostly no.
方向上与 Hao 等人一致、幅度更小：数学的集体注意力已流向最优化、数值与统计——正是驱动机器学习、也被其驱动的子领域， 即其“数据最丰富领域”在数学中的对应。纯分析与代数并未绝对萎缩，但相对份额在下降。关键问题——这是否因为 AI 时代而发生？——由下方检验回答，答案基本是否定的。

4 · Statistical tests — is this an AI story? 统计检验：这是 AI 的故事吗？

No break where AI would put one. A structural-break search over the monthly effective-subfields series lets the data pick the most likely break date. It picks — — squarely in the pandemic, not the LLM era — and even that candidate is not significant (sup-F —, block-bootstrap p = —): the decline is gradual, not event-shaped. Real, then flat. Multinomial bootstrap confidence intervals show the concentration is far beyond sampling noise — the 2017 and 2021 intervals don’t come close to overlapping — but the 2021 and 2025 intervals do overlap almost entirely: the contraction happened by 2021 and has since plateaued. Post-2023 levels sit — effective subfields below the pre-COVID trend projection — a persistent level shift, not a continuing slide. 多项自助置信区间显示，集中化远超抽样噪声——2017 与 2021 年的区间完全分离；但 2021 与 2025 年的区间几乎完全重叠： 收缩在 2021 年前已完成，此后趋于平稳。2023 年后的水平低于疫情前趋势外推值，是持续的水平位移，而非继续下滑。

Verdict / 结论. Mathematics’ focus contraction is real — and it is a pandemic-era story that persisted, not an LLM-era story. Where Hao et al. find AI adoption actively narrowing natural science, math’s narrowing predates ChatGPT and has not (yet) accelerated since. If AI is reshaping mathematics, it is so far holding the field at its COVID-era concentration rather than deepening it — a genuine contrast worth watching as 2026 completes.
数学的聚焦收缩是真实的——但它是一个延续至今的疫情时期故事，而非大模型时期的故事。Hao 等人发现 AI 的采用正在 主动收窄自然科学，而数学的收窄先于 ChatGPT，且此后（暂时）没有加速。如果说 AI 正在重塑数学，那么到目前为止， 它是把该领域维持在疫情期形成的集中度上，而非使其加深——这一对照值得在 2026 年完整数据出炉后继续观察。

5 · Caveats 局限与注意

  • This is a structural analog, not a replication: we use submission-volume distribution, not citations, authorship, or AI-augmentation detection. We show concentration, not its cause. 这是结构性类比而非复现；显示集中而非成因。
  • Counts include cross-lists, so subfields overlap and should not be summed; shares are relative measures. 计数含交叉列出，子领域有重叠，不应相加。
  • The data-cluster definition is a defensible heuristic, not a formal taxonomy; and cs.IT itself grew slowly (+9%), so the cluster’s rise is driven by optimization/numerics/statistics. 数据集群为启发式划分；信息论本身增长缓慢。
  • math.GM (General Mathematics) is a noisy catch-all and is excluded from the narrative despite high growth. 2026 is partial (Jan–Jun). 一般数学为杂项桶，已在叙述中排除；2026 年数据不完整。
  • Statistical caveats: only 5 treated categories in the DiD (cluster-robust SEs are conservative but few clusters is few clusters); the monthly entropy series is autocorrelated (addressed with a moving-block bootstrap, block = 12); “no significant break” bounds the evidence, it does not prove absence. 统计局限：双重差分仅 5 个处理组类别；月度熵序列存在自相关（以块自助法处理，块长 12）； “无显著断点”是证据的界定，并非不存在的证明。
