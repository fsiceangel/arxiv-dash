Focus Contraction in Mathematics? Timing, Mechanism, and the 2026 Inflection 数学的聚焦收缩？——时间、机制与 2026 年拐点

A comparative test of Hao, Xu, Li & Evans (Nature, 2026) on 32 arXiv mathematics subfields · Working paper, July 2026 · Data through June 2026 · Code & data: github.com/fsiceangel/arxiv-dash

Abstract
Hao, Xu, Li & Evans (Nature, 2026) report that AI adoption expands individual scientists’ impact while contracting science’s collective focus. We test whether mathematics exhibits the same contraction using three sources: monthly submission counts for 32 arXiv mathematics subfields (114 months, January 2017 – June 2026), paper-level metadata for 448,970 unique papers, and a 13-category non-mathematics control group. Concentration is real: the effective number of active subfields fell from 24.2 to 23.5 (multinomial-bootstrap 95% CIs disjoint), with growth concentrated in data-rich subfields (optimization +128%, numerical analysis +97%, statistics theory +95%, 2017→2025). Its timing, however, implicates the deep-learning era rather than the chat era: a structural-break search (candidate window through December 2024) selects October 2020 — itself insignificant (sup-F, block-bootstrap p = 0.43); the F-statistic at ChatGPT’s release is 1.68 against 5.41 at the COVID onset — concentration plateaued by 2021, and post-2022 event-study coefficients for the data-rich cluster are statistically indistinguishable from zero through 2025. Paper-level evidence supplies the mechanism: subfield growth correlates with rising AI-related content (Spearman ρ = 0.425, permutation p = 0.016), which quadrupled in the data-rich cluster (2.9% → 13.5%) with its steepest rise in 2017–2020, while inter-subfield co-listing rose — the opposite of the engagement decline reported for the natural sciences. Finally, we document a sharp, field-wide output surge beginning February 2026 (median +22.5% year-over-year against a +1.8% baseline; 30 of 32 subfields above +10%), led by combinatorics (z = 5.8) and logic rather than AI-adjacent subfields, and present across theoretical physics and non-AI computer science while absent in AI-core categories — consistent with generative AI arriving in theoretical science as a production technology rather than a research topic. End-of-sample predictive tests date an LLM-era break to 2026: output (F = 5.85) and concentration (F = 2.66) both deviate from the 2021–2025 plateau trend (p < 0.001). Mathematics thus contrasts with the natural-science pattern throughout the chat era; the LLM era’s imprint arrives in 2026, and its character — production-side and field-wide rather than data-seeking and topic-side — differs from the contraction described for the natural sciences.
摘要
Hao、Xu、Li 与 Evans（《自然》2026）报告：AI 的采用扩大了科学家个人的影响力，却收缩了科学整体的研究视野。 我们基于三类数据检验数学是否呈现同样的收缩：32 个 arXiv 数学子领域的每月投稿数（114 个月，2017 年 1 月–2026 年 6 月）、 448,970 篇唯一论文的元数据，以及 13 个非数学分类的对照组。集中化是真实的：活跃子领域的有效数目从 24.2 降至 23.5 （多项自助 95% 置信区间不相交），增长集中于数据密集子领域（最优化 +128%、数值分析 +97%、统计理论 +95%，2017→2025）。 但其时间点指向深度学习时代而非聊天模型时代：结构性断点搜索（候选窗口至 2024 年 12 月）选中 2020 年 10 月—— 其本身并不显著（sup-F，块自助 p = 0.43）；ChatGPT 发布时点的 F 统计量为 1.68，而疫情起点为 5.41—— 集中化在 2021 年前已趋平稳，数据密集集群 2022 年后直至 2025 年的事件研究系数与零无法区分。论文层面的证据提供了机制：子领域增长与 AI 相关内容的上升显著相关（Spearman ρ = 0.425， 置换检验 p = 0.016），数据密集集群的 AI 相关占比翻了两番（2.9% → 13.5%），且攀升最陡的阶段为 2017–2020； 与此同时子领域间共同列出上升——与自然科学报告的互动下降相反。最后，我们记录了 2026 年 2 月开始的一场急剧、 全领域的产出涌升（同比中位数 +22.5%，基线 +1.8%；32 个子领域中 30 个超过 +10%），由组合数学（z = 5.8）与数理逻辑 而非 AI 相邻子领域领跑，并同样出现在理论物理与非 AI 计算机科学中、却在 AI 核心分类中缺席—— 与生成式 AI 以生产技术而非研究主题的方式抵达理论科学相一致。样本末端预测检验将大模型时代的断点定位于 2026 年： 产出（F = 5.85）与集中度（F = 2.66）双双偏离 2021–2025 平台期趋势（p < 0.001）。因此，在聊天模型时代， 数学始终是自然科学模式的对照；大模型时代的印记于 2026 年到来，且其特征——生产端的、全领域的， 而非寻找数据的、主题端的——不同于自然科学所记录的收缩。

1 · Introduction 引言

Hao, Xu, Li & Evans [1] analyze 41.3 million natural-science papers, flagging AI-augmented research with a fine-tuned language model (F1 = 0.875 against expert labels). They document a paradox: AI expands individual impact — AI-augmented authors publish 3.02× more papers, earn 4.84× more citations, and lead projects 1.37 years earlier — yet AI adoption contracts collective science, shrinking the volume of topics studied by 4.63% and reducing inter-paper engagement by 22%. AI-augmented work migrates toward “areas richest in data,” forming “lonely crowds” that automate established fields rather than open new ones. Their corpus covers biology, medicine, chemistry, physics, materials science and geology; mathematics is not among the six disciplines studied, and it differs from all of them in the respect their mechanism turns on: most of mathematics has no data-rich empirical frontier for AI to exploit.

Hao、Xu、Li 与 Evans [1] 分析了 4130 万篇自然科学论文，用微调语言模型（对专家标注 F1 = 0.875）识别 AI 辅助研究， 并记录了一个悖论：AI 扩大了个人影响力——AI 辅助作者发表论文数为 3.02 倍、被引 4.84 倍、提前 1.37 年主持项目—— 却收缩了科学整体：研究主题总量减少 4.63%、论文间互动减少 22%。AI 辅助研究涌向“数据最丰富的领域”， 形成只自动化既有领域、不开拓新方向的“孤独人群”。其语料覆盖生物、医学、化学、物理、材料与地质六个学科； 数学不在其中——而数学恰恰在其机制的关键之处与这六者不同：数学的大部分领域并没有可供 AI 开采的数据密集型经验前沿。

This paper asks whether mathematics nonetheless exhibits the same collective contraction, and makes four contributions: (i) a descriptive account of concentration across the 32 arXiv mathematics subfields, 2017–2026 (§4.1); (ii) formal timing evidence — a structural-break search with bootstrap inference, bootstrap confidence intervals on annual concentration, and a two-way fixed-effects event study — showing the contraction is pandemic-era, not LLM-era (§4.2); (iii) paper-level mechanism and engagement evidence from 448,970 papers (§4.3); and (iv) documentation of a field-wide output surge beginning February 2026, benchmarked against a 13-category non-mathematics control group (§4.4). 本文检验数学是否仍呈现同样的整体收缩，并做出四点贡献：(i) 2017–2026 年 32 个 arXiv 数学子领域 集中化的描述性刻画（§4.1）；(ii) 正式的时间证据——带自助推断的结构性断点搜索、年度集中度的自助置信区间、 双向固定效应事件研究——表明收缩发生于疫情期而非大模型期（§4.2）；(iii) 来自 448,970 篇论文的 机制与互动证据（§4.3）；(iv) 记录 2026 年 2 月开始的全领域产出涌升，并以 13 个非数学分类对照组作基准（§4.4）。

2 · Data 数据

Monthly submission counts. For each of the 32 mathematics subject categories (the math.* set plus cs.IT and math-ph, following arXiv’s Mathematics listing) we record the “Total of N entries” figure from arXiv’s public monthly listing pages (arxiv.org/list/<cat>/YYYY-MM), January 2017 – June 2026: 32 × 114 = 3,648 category-months with no missing cells. Counts include cross-listed papers, so a paper contributes to every category it lists and categories must not be summed; all share-based measures are unaffected by this convention, and §4.3 reports a robustness check counting each paper once. 每月投稿数。对 32 个数学学科分类，我们记录 arXiv 公开每月列表页的“Total of N entries”， 2017 年 1 月–2026 年 6 月，共 3,648 个分类-月，无缺失。计数含交叉列出，论文会计入其列出的每个分类， 故各分类不可相加；基于份额的度量不受影响，§4.3 报告了每篇论文只计一次的稳健性检验。

Paper-level metadata. From the arXiv metadata snapshot [3] (retrieved 13 July 2026) we keep every paper listing at least one of the 32 subfields with a first-version date in 2017–2026: 448,970 unique papers with full category lists, primary category, title and abstract. Years are assigned by first-version date, so paper-level annual counts differ slightly from listing-page counts. 论文级元数据。取自 arXiv 元数据快照 [3]（2026 年 7 月 13 日获取），保留首版日期在 2017–2026 年、 列有 32 个子领域之一的全部论文：448,970 篇唯一论文，含完整分类、主分类、标题与摘要。年份按首版日期划分， 故与列表页计数略有差异。

Control group. Thirteen non-mathematics categories harvested identically (2019–2026): AI-core computer science (cs.LG, cs.CL, cs.CV); non-AI computer science (cs.CC, cs.DS, cs.PL, cs.LO); theoretical physics (hep-th, gr-qc, quant-ph, cond-mat.stat-mech); and economics/statistics (econ.TH, stat.ME). 对照组。以同样方法采集的 13 个非数学分类（2019–2026）：AI 核心计算机科学、非 AI 计算机科学、 理论物理与经济/统计。

3 · Methods 方法

Concentration measures. Let pi,t be subfield i’s share of all listings in period t. We report Shannon entropy Ht = −Σi pi,t log₂ pi,t and, as our headline measure, the effective number of subfields Et = 2Ht — the number of equally active subfields the field behaves like; falling Et means concentration. We also report Pielou evenness Jt = Ht/log₂ 32 [8], the Herfindahl–Hirschman index, the top-5 share, and the combined share of a pre-specified data-rich cluster (cs.IT, math.OC, math.NA, math.ST, math.PR) — the mathematics counterpart of “areas richest in data” [1]. 集中度度量。设 pi,t 为子领域 i 在期 t 的份额。我们报告香农熵 Ht 及主指标有效子领域数 Et = 2^Ht（下降即集中化），并报告 Pielou 均匀度 [8]、HHI、 前五份额，以及预先设定的数据密集集群（信息论、最优化、数值分析、统计理论、概率）合计份额—— 即文献 [1] 中“数据最丰富领域”在数学中的对应。

Structural-break search. On the monthly series Et (114 points) we fit Et = α + βt + 𝟙{t ≥ τ}(γ + δt) + εt and compute the Chow-type F-statistic for each candidate τ in the central 70% of the sample, taking the supremum (Quandt–Andrews [4,5]). Because εt is autocorrelated, the p-value for sup-F is obtained by a moving-block bootstrap (block length 12, B = 500, seed 42 [6]) under the single-trend null. We additionally report F(τ) at two hypothesized dates: the COVID onset (March 2020) and ChatGPT’s release (December 2022). 结构性断点搜索。在月度序列 Et 上拟合带断点的分段线性趋势，对样本中部 70% 的每个候选 断点计算 Chow 型 F 统计量并取上确界（Quandt–Andrews [4,5]）。因残差自相关，sup-F 的 p 值由无断点原假设下的 移动块自助法获得（块长 12，B = 500，种子 42 [6]）。另报告两个先验时点的 F 值：疫情起点（2020-03）与 ChatGPT 发布（2022-12）。

End-of-sample predictive-failure test. The sup-F trimming excludes the final 15% of the sample, so a break near the end cannot be detected by the search. We therefore fit the linear trend on the plateau window (January 2021 – December 2025) and apply a Chow-type predictive test to the six months of 2026: F is the mean squared out-of-sample deviation over the in-sample residual variance, with the p-value from a moving-block bootstrap of in-sample residuals (m = 6, B = 2,000, seed 42). The test is run on both Et and log total monthly output. 样本末端预测失效检验。sup-F 的截尾排除了样本最后 15%，故搜索无法探测靠近末端的断点。 为此我们在平台期窗口（2021 年 1 月–2025 年 12 月）拟合线性趋势，对 2026 年的六个月做 Chow 型预测检验： F 为样本外均方偏离与样本内残差方差之比，p 值来自样本内残差的移动块自助法（m = 6，B = 2,000，种子 42）。 检验同时施加于 Et 与对数月度总产出。

Bootstrap confidence intervals. For each year we resample the 32-category count vector from a multinomial with that year’s total and shares (B = 2,000, seed 42) and report percentile 95% intervals for E. 自助置信区间。逐年按当年总数与份额做多项重抽样（B = 2,000，种子 42）， 报告 E 的百分位 95% 区间。

Difference-in-differences and event study. On log monthly counts (log max(n,1)) we estimate log nc,t = αc + λt + β·(Dc×Postt) + θ·(Dc×Covidt) + εc,t, where Dc marks the 5-category data-rich cluster, Postt = 𝟙{t ≥ Dec 2022}, Covidt = 𝟙{Mar 2020 ≤ t ≤ Dec 2021}; αc, λt are category and month fixed effects. Standard errors are clustered by category (G = 32, small-sample factor G/(G−1)) [7]. The event study replaces Post with year dummies interacted with Dc, base year 2022. 双重差分与事件研究。对对数月度计数估计双向固定效应模型：类别与月份固定效应， 处理组为 5 个数据密集分类，Post 为 2022 年 12 月起，另含疫情期交互控制；标准误按类别聚类（G = 32， 小样本修正 G/(G−1)）[7]。事件研究以 2022 年为基准年，用逐年交互项替代 Post。

AI-content classifier. A paper is flagged AI-related if its title or abstract matches any of the following (case-insensitive except LLM): machine learning · deep learning · neural network · neural operator · reinforcement learning · artificial intelligence · language model · generative adversarial · autoencoder · graph neural · deep neural · convolutional neural · physics-informed neural · attention mechanism · transformer (model|network|architecture) · \bLLMs?\b This is deliberately conservative and measures AI as topic; it cannot detect AI used as a tool (§4.4). Per subfield-year we compute the AI-related share; the growth–adoption association is the correlation between a subfield’s 2017→2025 growth and its change in AI share, with two-sided permutation p-values (10,000 draws, seed 42). AI 内容分类器。标题或摘要命中上述关键词之一即标记为 AI 相关（除 LLM 外不区分大小写）。 该分类器刻意保守，度量“作为主题的 AI”，无法识别“作为工具的 AI”（§6）。按子领域-年计算 AI 相关占比； 增长与采用的关联为子领域 2017→2025 增长与其 AI 占比变化的相关系数，p 值来自双侧置换检验（10,000 次，种子 42）。

Engagement. For each paper we count the subfields it lists among the 32; we track the yearly mean, the share listing ≥ 2, and the effective number of distinct subfield pairs 2H(pairs). This is a co-listing notion of engagement — weaker than the citation-based measure of [1], but computable without citation data. 互动。统计每篇论文列出的子领域数：年均值、跨 ≥2 个子领域的占比、以及子领域对的有效数目。 这是基于共同列出的互动概念——弱于文献 [1] 的引用度量，但无需引用数据即可计算。

2026 surge scores. For each category, the H1 (January–June) year-over-year log ratio for 2026 is z-scored against that category’s own H1-YoY history (mathematics: 2018–2025; controls: 2020–2025). The same statistic is computed identically for the 13 control categories. 2026 涌升分数。对每个分类，将 2026 年上半年同比对数比率相对该分类自身的上半年同比历史 （数学：2018–2025；对照：2020–2025）标准化为 z 分数；对照组按完全相同方法计算。

All analyses use Python 3.12 / NumPy 2.2 with fixed seeds; the pipeline (harvest.py, analyze.py, stats_analysis.py, process_metadata.py, controls.py) is in the repository. 全部分析基于 Python 3.12 / NumPy 2.2，随机种子固定；完整流程见仓库脚本。

Interpretation / 解读. Directionally like Hao et al., at smaller magnitude: math’s collective attention has drifted toward optimization, numerics and statistics — the subfields that power, and are powered by, machine learning; the math counterpart of their “areas richest in data.” Pure analysis and algebra are not shrinking in absolute terms, but they have lost relative share. The crucial question — did this happen because of the AI era? — is answered in §4.2, and the answer is mostly no.
方向上与 Hao 等人一致、幅度更小：数学的集体注意力已流向最优化、数值与统计——正是驱动机器学习、也被其驱动的子领域， 即其“数据最丰富领域”在数学中的对应。纯分析与代数并未绝对萎缩，但相对份额在下降。关键问题——这是否因为 AI 时代而发生？——由下方检验回答，答案基本是否定的。

4.2 · Timing: is the contraction an AI-era event? 时间检验：收缩是 AI 时代的事件吗？

No break where chat-era AI would put one. A structural-break search over the monthly effective-subfields series lets the data pick the most likely break date. It picks — — squarely in the pandemic, not the LLM era — and even that candidate is not significant (sup-F —, block-bootstrap p = —): through 2025, the decline is gradual, not event-shaped. Forcing the break at ChatGPT’s release scores F = —, far weaker than the COVID-era candidate’s F = —. 对月度有效子领域序列做结构性断点搜索，让数据自行选择最可能的断点日期：结果落在疫情期而非大模型期， 且该候选断点本身亦不显著——就 2025 年及之前而言，下降是渐进的，而非事件式的。将断点强行放在 ChatGPT 发布日， 其 F 值远弱于疫情期候选。

Through 2024 — but not beyond. By construction the search trims the final 15% of candidates, so its verdict covers break dates through — only; it is silent about the end of the sample. 4.4 · The 2026 inflection and the control group 2026 年拐点与对照组

Something changed in February 2026. Year-over-year, first-half 2026 submissions are up in 30 of 32 subfields by more than 10%, with a median of +22.5% — against a +1.8% median the year before. The onset is visible from February and strengthens through June. And the leaders are not the data-rich cluster: Combinatorics (+41%), Logic (+38%) and Dynamical Systems (+35%) head the list — the surge is field-wide, which is precisely why §4.2’s cluster-vs-rest design could not see it (a shift common to all subfields is absorbed by the month fixed effects; only the cluster’s extra +8% surfaced there). 2026 年 2 月起出现了变化。2026 年上半年同比，32 个子领域中有 30 个增长超过 10%， 中位数达 +22.5%——而前一年同期中位数仅 +1.8%。增长自 2 月起可见并逐月增强。且领跑者并非数据密集集群： 组合数学（+41%）、数理逻辑（+38%）、动力系统（+35%）居前——这是一场全领域的涌升， 这也正是 §4.2 的“集群对比其余”设计无法察觉它的原因（全领域共同的变化被月份固定效应吸收，只有集群相对多出的 +8% 显现）。

The inflection is not only about volume. Two further 2026 signals: (i) concentration deviates too — H1 2026 sits 0.39 effective subfields below the 2021–2025 plateau trend (predictive p < 0.001, §4.2), so the concentration dynamics that stalled in 2021 show first signs of resuming; and (ii) AI as a topic is now spreading into the pure subfields: the non-cluster AI-related share rose from 1.38% to 1.57% in half a year — the largest increment on record — with Category Theory jumping from 1.2% to 3.7% (Fig 9, top rows vs bottom rows). Summary / 小结. §4.2–4.3’s conclusion — a deep-learning-era concentration that plateaued — described 2017–2025 accurately. But the plateau ended in February 2026: a broad, accelerating, field-wide output surge that our subfield-level breakdown dates precisely and that is not concentrated in the historically AI-heavy subfields. If this is the LLM era arriving in mathematics, it is arriving everywhere at once — as a production technology, not a topic. The control group widens the lens: the same anomaly appears across theoretical CS, physics and statistics while AI’s own categories sit on trend — math is one instance of a surge across theoretical science. The predictive test already shows concentration deviating alongside output; 2026’s complete data will show whether that persists.
§4.2–4.3 的结论——深度学习时代的集中化随后进入平台期——准确描述了 2017–2025。但该平台期已于 2026 年 2 月结束： 一场广泛、加速、全领域的产出涌升，分领域拆解将其时间精确定位，且它并不集中于历史上 AI 浓度高的子领域。 如果这是大模型时代抵达数学，它是以生产技术而非研究主题的方式同时抵达所有领域。 对照组进一步拓宽了视野：同样的异常出现在理论计算机、物理与统计之中，而 AI 自身的分类却处于趋势线上—— 数学只是理论科学整体涌升的一个实例。预测检验已显示集中度与产出一同偏离；2026 年完整数据将检验其是否持续。

5 · Discussion 讨论

Three findings organize the evidence. First, mathematics did concentrate — the effective number of subfields fell ~3% with disjoint confidence intervals, growth accrued disproportionately to the data-rich cluster, and the pattern survives primary-category deduplication. Second, every timing instrument places that concentration in 2017–2021 — the deep-learning era — not after ChatGPT: the break search finds no significant break anywhere and prefers late 2020 to late 2022 by a factor of three in F; annual concentration is statistically flat 2021–2025; post-2022 event-study coefficients are zero; and the AI-content series that plausibly drove the shift rose fastest in 2017–2020. Third, mathematics lacks the engagement collapse central to the natural-science account [1]: co-listing between subfields rose while concentration proceeded. On the evidence through 2025, mathematics is a counterexample to the generality of the contraction mechanism — a field can absorb AI, concentrate toward its data-rich subfields, and yet become more internally connected. 证据由三项发现组织。其一，数学确实集中化了——有效子领域数下降约 3% 且置信区间不相交， 增长不成比例地流向数据密集集群，并且该模式在主分类去重后依然成立。其二，所有时间工具都将该集中化定位于 2017–2021（深度学习时代）而非 ChatGPT 之后：断点搜索在任何位置都未发现显著断点，且其对 2020 年末的偏好在 F 值上 三倍于 2022 年末；年度集中度在 2021–2025 间统计上持平；2022 年后事件研究系数为零；而可能驱动此变化的 AI 内容序列 攀升最快的阶段是 2017–2020。其三，数学缺少自然科学叙事 [1] 的核心要素——互动崩塌：集中化推进的同时， 子领域间共同列出反而上升。就 2025 年及之前的证据而言，数学是该收缩机制普适性的反例—— 一个领域可以吸收 AI、向数据密集子领域集中，却在内部变得更加互联。

The February 2026 inflection reopens the question — and answers the natural objection that “no LLM-era break” sits oddly with the visibility of AI-for-mathematics in 2025–26. The break exists; it is simply not where the natural-science chronology would place it. Its signature — sudden, field-wide, largest in pen-and-paper subfields, shared by theoretical physics and non-AI computer science, absent in AI-core categories — fits a change in how theoretical research is produced rather than in what it is about, and its timing coincides with the public arrival of reasoning-grade AI for mathematics (formalization systems and LLM-assisted problem solving) rather than with chat assistants. Two accompanying signals — the resumption of concentration (−0.39 effective subfields vs the plateau trend) and the record spread of AI-as-topic into pure subfields — suggest the composition of mathematics, not just its volume, is beginning to move again. If LLM assistance is the cause, the natural-science playbook may not predict what follows: the first-order effect in mathematics is output expansion everywhere, not migration toward data. Distinguishing the tool channel from alternatives (arXiv-wide policy shifts, submission behavior) requires paper-level evidence beyond metadata — full-text and acknowledgment analysis, and citation-based engagement — which we leave to future work, along with embedding-based topic-volume measurement to make our concentration estimates directly comparable to the 4.63% figure of [1]. 2026 年 2 月的拐点重启了这一问题——也回应了一个自然的质疑：“没有大模型时代断点”与 2025–26 年 AI4Math 的火热景象似乎相悖。断点是存在的，只是不在自然科学时间线所预期的位置。其特征——突发、全领域、 在纸笔子领域最强、同现于理论物理与非 AI 计算机科学、在 AI 核心分类缺席——符合理论研究生产方式的变化， 而非研究主题的变化；其时点与面向数学的推理级 AI（形式化系统与 LLM 辅助解题）的公开亮相重合， 而非与聊天助手重合。两个伴随信号——集中化的恢复（相对平台期趋势 −0.39 个有效子领域）与 AI 主题向纯数学子领域 的创纪录扩散——表明数学的构成而不仅是数量正重新开始变动。若 LLM 辅助是其原因，自然科学的剧本未必能预测后续： AI 在数学中的一阶效应是全面的产出扩张，而非向数据的迁移。 区分工具渠道与替代解释（arXiv 整体政策变化、投稿行为）需要元数据之外的论文级证据——全文与致谢分析、 基于引用的互动度量——我们将其与基于嵌入的主题体积测量（使我们的集中度估计可与 [1] 的 4.63% 直接比较）一并留作后续工作。

6 · Limitations 局限

  • This is a structural analog, not a replication: we use submission-volume distribution, not citations, authorship, or AI-augmentation detection. We show concentration, not its cause. 这是结构性类比而非复现；显示集中而非成因。
  • Counts include cross-lists, so subfields overlap and should not be summed; shares are relative measures. 计数含交叉列出，子领域有重叠，不应相加。
  • The data-cluster definition is a defensible heuristic, not a formal taxonomy; and cs.IT itself grew slowly (+9%), so the cluster’s rise is driven by optimization/numerics/statistics. 数据集群为启发式划分；信息论本身增长缓慢。
  • math.GM (General Mathematics) is a noisy catch-all and is excluded from the narrative despite high growth. 2026 is partial (Jan–Jun). 一般数学为杂项桶，已在叙述中排除；2026 年数据不完整。
  • Statistical caveats: only 5 treated categories in the DiD (cluster-robust SEs are conservative but few clusters is few clusters); the monthly entropy series is autocorrelated (addressed with a moving-block bootstrap, block = 12); “no significant break” bounds the evidence, it does not prove absence. 统计局限：双重差分仅 5 个处理组类别；月度熵序列存在自相关（以块自助法处理，块长 12）； “无显著断点”是证据的界定，并非不存在的证明。
  • §4.3: the AI classifier is a conservative keyword list, not Hao et al.’s fine-tuned language model (F1 = 0.875) — it undercounts subtle AI usage and measures AI as topic more than AI as tool; co-listing is a weaker engagement notion than their citation-based measure; metadata uses each paper’s first-version date, so yearly counts differ slightly from the listing-page counts. §4.3：AI 分类器为保守的关键词列表而非其微调语言模型（F1 = 0.875），会低估隐性 AI 使用， 且更多度量“作为主题的 AI”而非“作为工具的 AI”；共同列出弱于其基于引用的互动度量； 元数据按论文首版日期计年，与列表页计数略有差异。
  • §4.4: H1 2026 is compared like-for-like against H1 of prior years, but the year is incomplete and late cross-lists still accrue. The control group shows the surge is arXiv-wide across theory fields, which rules out math-specific causes but not an arXiv-wide policy change — though the AI-core categories sitting on/below trend argues against a pure policy artifact. The surge z-scores are computed against short baseline histories (8 H1-YoY observations for mathematics, 6 for controls), so they order the anomalies reliably but their absolute magnitudes should be read qualitatively. The AI-as-tool interpretation remains a hypothesis, not a measured result. §4.4：2026 上半年与往年上半年同口径比较，但年份不完整且交叉列出仍在累积。 对照组显示涌升在理论学科间普遍存在，排除了数学特有的原因，但不能完全排除 arXiv 整体政策变化—— 不过 AI 核心分类处于或低于自身趋势，不支持纯政策性假象。涌升 z 分数基于较短的基线历史（数学 8 个、对照 6 个 上半年同比观测），其排序可靠，但绝对数值应作定性解读。“作为工具的 AI”解释仍是假说，而非已测量的结果。
